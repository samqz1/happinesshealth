About Happiness Health

At Happiness Health, we are passionate about guiding individuals, children, and families across Maryland toward lasting health and happiness through compassionate, personalized mental health care. Our team of licensed professionals brings together decades of clinical experience and leadership to offer expert therapy, counseling, and coaching tailored to your unique needs. We specialize in evidence-based approaches, including Brainspotting and EMDR therapy, to help you heal and thrive.

Why Choose Us?

  • Diverse Expertise: Trauma treatment, substance use recovery, and psychotherapy for all ages.
  • Culturally Sensitive Care: Inclusive support from multicultural and multilingual therapists.
  • Proven Methods: Evidence-based approaches including TFCBT, AFCBT, and MRT.
  • Comprehensive Services: Therapy, life coaching, and corporate mental health & wellness consulting.

Whether you're seeking personal healing or workplace wellness solutions, we're here to support your growth and well-being—every step of the way.

Our Approaches

Behavioral Therapy

Focused on changing harmful behaviors through positive reinforcement and strategies.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Helps identify and change negative thought patterns to improve mental health.

Family/Marital Therapy

Strengthens relationships through improved communication and conflict resolution.

Multicultural

Culturally sensitive approaches tailored to diverse backgrounds and experiences.

Brainspotting

A powerful method for processing trauma and emotional pain.

Eye Movement Desensitization & Reprocessing Therapy (EMDR)

Effective for trauma recovery and reducing distressing memories.

Trauma Focused

Specialized care to address and heal from traumatic experiences.

Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy (MBCT)

Combines mindfulness practices with cognitive therapy techniques.

Psychotherapy

A collaborative approach to explore thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.

SPARCS Therapy

Structured therapy for adolescents dealing with trauma and stress.

Moral Recognition Therapy (MRT)

Focuses on moral reasoning to promote positive behavioral changes.

Attachment-Based

Strengthens emotional bonds and addresses attachment-related challenges.
